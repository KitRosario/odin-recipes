Hot Chocolate

Hot Chocolate is warm and delicious!

Recipe

  • dark chocolate chips
  • milk
  • heavy cream
  • salt

Directions

  1. Bring the milk to a slight boil
  2. Slowly add the chocolate chips into the milk
  3. Continually whisk the chocolate chips until they have completely melted
  4. Sprinkle some salt and add a hint of heavy cream
  5. Serve
