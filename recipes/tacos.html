Tacos

Tacos for everyone!

Recipe

  • Ground Beef
  • Taco seasoning packets
  • Tortillas
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Limes

Directions

  1. Brown the ground beef
  2. Add the Taco seasoning packet and mix
  3. Warm the tortillas in a pan
  4. Add the beef on the tortillas and sprinkle cheese on top
  5. Squeeze fresh lime on top and Serve
